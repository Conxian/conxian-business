# Vision & Mission

## Vision
To establish a future where human discretion is replaced by mathematical certainty, anchored in the sovereignty of Bitcoin and the absolute authority of code.

## Mission
Conxian Labs builds the infrastructure for Sovereign Autonomous Businesses (SAB). We enable trustless, non-custodial financial operations by tying system states to the Bitcoin burn-block height, ensuring that "Code is Law."

## Core Principle
**Code is Law.**
