# 5-Year Strategic Plan

## Timeline Overview
- **Year 1-2**: Foundation & Product-Market Fit. $1.5M Pre-seed focus.
- **Year 2-3**: Scaling Operations & Market Expansion. $15M Seed focus.
- **Year 3-4**: Series A & Infrastructure Hardening. $50M target.
- **Year 5+**: Global Dominance & Series C. $100M+ target.

## Infrastructure Strategy
- **Years 0-3.5**: Cloud-only infrastructure to maintain agility and minimize capital expenditure.
- **Year 3.5+**: Evaluation of hybrid/private cloud for extreme sovereignty and scale.

## Scaling Triggers
- Scaling is triggered by monthly revenue milestones and system telemetry.
