# Funding Tiers & Milestones

## Tier 1: Pre-seed ($1.5M)
- **Focus**: Core team formation, MVP of Conxius Platform, Initial partner integrations.
- **Milestone**: Successful deployment of non-custodial gateway with Rust/Actix-web.

## Tier 2: Seed ($15M)
- **Focus**: Expansion of the Conxian UI dashboard, Global marketing, Institutional pilots.
- **Milestone**: Achieving initial monthly revenue targets and user growth metrics.

## Tier 3: Series A ($50M)
- **Focus**: Infrastructure hardening, Compliance certifications, Strategic acquisitions.
- **Milestone**: Completion of the initial 3.5-year cloud-only phase.

## Tier 4: Series C ($100M+)
- **Focus**: Global scale, Sovereign Autonomous Business logic ecosystem.
- **Milestone**: Full transition to SAB logic for all partner operations.
